Physics is a Human Story

Before equations, there were people — curious, stubborn, brilliant people who changed how we see the world. These are their stories.

Read these not as history lessons but as examples of what it looks like to think carefully, question assumptions, and push through confusion until something becomes clear. Every physicist you will study had a moment where nothing made sense — and kept going anyway.
