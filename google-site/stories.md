# STORIES OF SCIENCE

## Page Header
**Title:** Stories of Science
**Subtitle:** Physics is not a list of equations. It is a record of human curiosity.

---

## Opening paragraph
Behind every law of physics is a person — someone who looked at the world and refused to accept that it could not be understood. These are their stories.

Read them not as history lessons but as examples of what it looks like to think carefully, question assumptions, and push through confusion until something becomes clear.
