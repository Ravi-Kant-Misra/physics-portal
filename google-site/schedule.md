## Weekly Structure

| Day | What happens |
|---|---|
| **Lesson Day** | New concept introduced. Lesson HTML reviewed together. Questions welcomed. |
| **Classwork Day** | Classwork problems completed in class. Teacher available throughout. |
| **Homework Due** | Completed homework submitted via the portal before the next session. |

---

## What to Bring Every Session
- Your device (to access the portal)
- A pen and paper for working — physics is always written by hand first
- Your previous unit's homework if corrections were requested

---

## Pacing

Each unit takes approximately:
- **15–20 minutes** to read the lesson
- **20–30 minutes** to complete classwork
- **20–30 minutes** to complete homework independently

There is no race. The next unit unlocks when the current one is genuinely understood and approved — not before.

---

## Holidays and Missed Sessions
If you miss a session, the portal is always available. Lessons and classwork can be completed independently. Reach out if you need support.
