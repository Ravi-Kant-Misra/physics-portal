# HOW TO BUILD THE GOOGLE SITE
# Physics Foundation — where Concepts Click
# Estimated time: 25–30 minutes

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
STEP 1 — CREATE THE SITE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Go to https://sites.google.com
2. Click the + (New Site) button
3. Click on "Untitled Site" at the top left → type:
   Physics Foundation
4. Click on "Your page title" in the main area → type:
   where Concepts Click

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
STEP 2 — SET THE HEADER
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Click on the header area at the top of the page
2. Click "Change image" → choose a dark blue or space image
   (or search "Upload" and use a plain dark blue colour)
3. Below the title, click "Add subheading" → type:
   Think Deeper. Learn Better. — with Ravi M.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
STEP 3 — HOME PAGE CONTENT
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

--- Section 1: Welcome text ---
From the right panel click INSERT → Text box
Paste this text:

"This is your home for IB Physics — a structured, step-by-step journey where every concept is built carefully before the next one begins.

Physics is not about memorising formulas. It is about understanding why the universe behaves the way it does. When you understand the concept, the formula writes itself."

--- Section 2: Three feature cards ---
From INSERT panel → click "3 columns" layout
Fill in each column:

Column 1:
📖 Learn at Your Own Pace
Work through lessons, classwork, and homework unit by unit. Each chapter unlocks only when the previous one is complete.

Column 2:
✅ Parent-Reviewed Progress
Every homework submission is reviewed by your parent against the answer key before your next chapter unlocks.

Column 3:
🚀 Built on Strong Foundations
We start with the mathematical tools you need — significant figures, vectors, graphs — before diving into physics.

--- Section 3: Portal button ---
From INSERT panel → Button
Label: Enter Student Portal →
Link: https://script.google.com/macros/s/AKfycbwc0-62SrefMlpwxJtBXZN1ud4clOT0CC9wSgzndq4n6XC2z-5HrlE_idQ6OhSv1WxJsQ/exec

--- Section 4: Stories teaser ---
Add a text box:
"Physics is a Human Story

Before equations, there were people — curious, stubborn, brilliant people who changed how we see the world."

Add a button:
Label: Read the Stories →
Link: (this will link to the Stories page — come back and add after creating that page)

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
STEP 4 — ADD PAGES
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

In the right panel click PAGES → click + Add page

Create these 5 pages in this order:
1. Stories of Science
2. Rules of Engagement
3. My Vision
4. The Ideal Student
5. Schedule

STEP 10 — NAVIGATION
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Google Sites builds the navigation automatically from your pages.
Check the top of the site — you should see:
Home | Stories of Science | Rules of Engagement | My Vision | The Ideal Student | Schedule

If the order is wrong: PAGES panel → drag pages into this order.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
STEP 11 — PUBLISH
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Click PUBLISH (top right)
2. URL will be: sites.google.com/view/physics-foundation
   (or similar — Google Sites picks it automatically)
3. Under "Who can view this site" → select "Anyone"
4. Click PUBLISH

Your site is now live. Share this URL with students and parents.
That is the only link they ever need.
