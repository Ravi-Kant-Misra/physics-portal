Part C

Q13: Possible: 36.8, 37.0, 37.4 (any value between 36.5 and 37.5). Not possible: e.g. 37.6 (greater than 37+0.5=37.5).
Q14: The ± symbol means "plus or minus" — the true value could be anywhere from a−Δa up to a+Δa. Δa is always positive because it represents a distance (half-width of the range), not a direction. A negative uncertainty would make no physical sense.
