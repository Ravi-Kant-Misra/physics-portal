Part C

Q14. Step 1 (addition): 4.5 + 2.35 = 6.85 → limited to 1 dp (4.5) → use 6.9 for the sig fig count (2 sf), but carry 6.85 through.
Step 2 (multiplication): 6.85 × 3.0 — 3.0 has 2 sf, 6.85 treated as 2 sf → 6.85 × 3.0 = 20.55 → round to 2 sf = 21
Q15. 12.34 has 4 sig figs. 7.1 has 2 sig figs — this is the limiting number. The answer must be rounded to 2 sig figs. 87.654 → 88. Writing 87.654 implies 5 sig figs of precision, which is dishonest because 7.1 was only measured to 2 sig figs.
