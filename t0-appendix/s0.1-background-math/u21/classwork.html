Reference — Keep these rules in mind!

1
Vector rule: →A = →Ax + →Ay   (every vector = its x-component + y-component, added vectorially)
2
Pythagorean rule: Ax² + Ay² = A²   (magnitudes form a right triangle — A is the hypotenuse)
!
WARNING: A ≠ Ax + Ay for diagonal vectors. Always use the Pythagorean theorem!
