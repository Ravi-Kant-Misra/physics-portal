Rules Reminder

  1. The farthest-left non-zero digit is the most significant figure.
  2. No decimal point → farthest-right non-zero digit is the least significant figure.
  3. Decimal point present → farthest-right digit is least significant — even if it is zero.
  4. All digits between most and least significant are significant.

Part C

Q16. 500 has no decimal point so only the 5 is significant (1 sig fig). 500. has a decimal point which signals the zeros were actually measured — giving 3 sig figs. The decimal point is a deliberate mark of precision.

Q17. No, they are not the same. 1.20 m has 3 sig figs — the trailing zero shows measurement to the nearest cm. 1.2 m has 2 sig figs — only to the nearest 10 cm. The extra zero carries real information about precision.
