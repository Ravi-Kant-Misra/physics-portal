Answer Key

Part A — Full Reductions

Q1 — Pascal
kg·m⁻¹·s⁻²
kg·m·s⁻² ÷ m² = kg·m⁻¹·s⁻²
Q2 — Joule (E=mgh)
kg·m²·s⁻²
kg × m·s⁻² × m = kg·m²·s⁻²
Q3 — Volt (V=IR)
kg·m²·s⁻³·A⁻¹
A × kg·m²·s⁻³·A⁻² = kg·m²·s⁻³·A⁻¹
Q4 — Ohm (R=P/I²)
kg·m²·s⁻³·A⁻²
kg·m²·s⁻³ ÷ A² = kg·m²·s⁻³·A⁻²

Part C — Challenge

Q9: V = E/q = (kg·m²·s⁻²) / (A·s) = kg·m²·s⁻²·A⁻¹·s⁻¹ = kg·m²·s⁻³·A⁻¹ ✓
Q10: density = mass/volume = kg / m³ = kg·m⁻³
Q11: B = F/(qv) = kg·m·s⁻² / (A·s × m·s⁻¹) = kg·m·s⁻² / (A·m) = kg·s⁻²·A⁻¹ = kg·s⁻²·A⁻¹ (this is the Tesla, T)
