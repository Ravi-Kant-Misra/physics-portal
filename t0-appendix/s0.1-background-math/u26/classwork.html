Part D — Concept Check (Sample Answers)

Q17: sin(θ) takes an angle as input and outputs a ratio of sides (Opp/Hyp). sin⁻¹(θ) is the inverse — it takes a ratio of sides as input and outputs an angle. They undo each other.
Q18: No. tan⁻¹ requires the Opposite and Adjacent sides. Here we have Opposite and Hypotenuse — that requires sin⁻¹(3/4) = 48.6°.
Q19: Press SHIFT (or 2nd) → cos → type 0.6 → close bracket ) → press = (or EXE). The answer is 53.1°.
